🕵️ The Lost Treasure Hunt

There are 5 mysterious riddles hidden across southern Tunisia! Guess each answer to win 10 points per riddle!

Riddle 1 🧩 🔒 Locked

"I am an ancient Berber fortress near Tataouine. My four-story stone rooms store grain and secrets. Tourists call me one of the most beautiful ksour in the world. What am I?"

Riddle 2 🧩 🔒 Locked

"I am a giant salt lake in southern Tunisia. In summer I dry up and look like a giant mirror of dazzling salt and light. Cars cross a road that seems to disappear into me. What am I?"

Riddle 3 🧩 🔒 Locked

"I am a tiny desert animal with ears bigger than my head! I can hear a mouse from 300 metres away. I live in the Sahara dunes of southern Tunisia. What am I?"

Riddle 4 🧩 🔒 Locked

"I am a city nicknamed the 'Gateway to the Sahara'. Every December I host the world's biggest desert festival with camel racing. I am surrounded by golden dunes. What city am I?"

Riddle 5 🧩 🔒 Locked

"I am a beautiful island in southern Tunisia. I was the 'Tatooine' planet in Star Wars! My white houses and blue sea look like paradise. My famous Deglet Nour dates are the best in the world. What city am I near?"
