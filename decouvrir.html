Discover Southern Tunisia

Browse the amazing landmarks, natural wonders, and fun places of the beautiful Tunisian South!

🏰 Ancient Berber Fortress

Ksar Ouled Soltane — A Living Castle

This incredible 600-year-old fortress near Tataouine was built by Berber nomads to store their grain and protect their food. Its beautiful four-story stone rooms (ghorfas) form a jaw-dropping courtyard!

🌀 Underground Berber Homes

Matmata — The Star Wars Village

The Berber people of Matmata dug their homes underground to escape the desert heat! These amazing cave houses were filmed as Luke Skywalker's home in Star Wars. You can still sleep in one today!

🧱 Traditional Brick Architecture

Tozeur Old Medina — Patterns in Clay

Tozeur's ancient medina is famous for its unique yellow brick buildings decorated with beautiful geometric patterns. No two walls look the same! The city was built over 1,000 years ago by Arab settlers in the oasis.

⛰️ Berber Mountain Village

Chenini — Village in the Sky

Perched on top of a dramatic rocky hill near Tataouine, Chenini is one of Tunisia's most stunning villages. Its white mosque and cave homes have been there for over 800 years, looking out over the vast Saharan landscape!

🏝️ Island of Heritage

Djerba — The Island of Dreams

The biggest island in North Africa has been inhabited for over 3,000 years! Its medina has ancient mosques, a famous Jewish synagogue, traditional pottery markets, and colorful street art. Connected to the mainland by a Roman causeway!

🪞 Giant Salt Lake

Chott el-Jerid — The Mirror Desert

This giant salt lake is as big as a small country! In summer it dries up and creates dazzling mirages that look like water. In winter, flamingos wade in its shallow edges. The road crossing it looks like it leads into infinity!

🏜️ Golden Sand Dunes

The Sahara Desert — Sea of Gold

In Douz you can touch the edge of the world's largest desert! Ride friendly camels, sandboard down golden dunes, and meet the adorable Fennec Fox with its enormous ears. Watching the sunset here is a memory for life!

💧 Waterfall in the Desert

Tamerza & Chebika — Secret Oases

Hidden in the Atlas mountain foothills, these magical oases have real waterfalls in the middle of the desert! Colorful birds, lush palms, and crystal-clear springs create a paradise that feels impossible — but it's real!

🦌 Rare Desert Animals

Bou Hedma National Park — Tunisia's Safari

Did you know Tunisia has its own safari? Bou Hedma is home to giant ostriches, rare slender-horned gazelles (Rhim), and wild oryxes roaming freely among ancient acacia trees — the oldest trees in North Africa!

🦩 Pink Flamingo Lagoon

Djerba Lagoon — Where Flamingos Dance

The shallow lagoons around Djerba island are home to hundreds of pink flamingos! These elegant birds arrive from Europe every year to feed on tiny shrimp that give them their famous pink color. A truly magical sight!

🐫 Desert Adventure

Camel Rides in Douz — Desert Style!

In Douz, climb aboard a friendly camel and ride into the real Sahara! Camels are gentle, funny animals that sway as they walk. You'll feel like a real desert nomad as you cross the golden dunes with the hot sun above!

🐊 Wildlife Encounter

Djerba Explore — Crocodiles & Museums!

This amazing park in Djerba has a massive crocodile farm with over 400 Nile crocodiles of all sizes! Plus a fantastic museum showing traditional Tunisian life through life-size reconstructions. Kids absolutely love it!

⭐ Movie Magic

Star Wars Trail — Walk on Tatooine!

The planet "Tatooine" in Star Wars was actually filmed in southern Tunisia! Visit Matmata where Luke Skywalker's home was filmed, and Mos Espa set near Tozeur. Stand in the exact spots where the famous scenes were shot!

🏖️ Island Fun

Djerba Beaches — Crystal-Clear Paradise!

Djerba's beaches have some of the most beautiful turquoise water in the Mediterranean! White sand, warm sea, water parks, banana boats, and pedalos — Djerba is the perfect island holiday destination for families!

🌴 Oasis Experience

Tozeur Oasis Walk — Date Tasting!

Walk through the magical palm grove of Tozeur — over 200,000 trees! Try the world-famous Deglet Nour dates fresh from the tree, ride a small carriage through the oasis, and watch how farmers harvest dates by climbing the palms!
